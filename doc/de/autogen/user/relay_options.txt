* [[option_relay.network.allowed_ips]] *relay.network.allowed_ips*
** Beschreibung: `regular expression with IPs allowed to use relay (case insensitive, use "(?-i)" at beginning to make it case sensitive); if IPv6 is enabled and that connection is made using IPv4, it will be IPv4-mapped IPv6 address (like: "::ffff:127.0.0.1"), example: "^((::ffff:)?123.45.67.89|192.160.*)$"`
** Typ: Zeichenkette
** Werte: beliebige Zeichenkette (Standardwert: `""`)

** Typ: Zeichenkette
** Werte: beliebige Zeichenkette (Standardwert: `""`)
